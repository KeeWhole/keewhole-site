Our story

One shop. Eight categories. Zero runaround.

Kee Whole was built on a simple frustration — Nairobi car owners had to visit five different shops to sort out their mats, stereo, tinting, stickers and bike gear. We fixed that. One location in Parklands, everything handled, everyone served.

Who we are

Born in Parklands, built for Nairobi

Kee Whole started as Smart Autos — a small operation in Parklands focused on car accessories. As the business grew, so did the categories: from floor mats and stereo installations to window tinting, PPF, vinyl decal stickers, phone skins, bicycle accessories and full frame wraps.

The rebrand to Kee Whole wasn't just cosmetic. It was a declaration — that everything a car owner, cyclist, or device user needs should orbit around one trusted shop. The name and logo say it all: Cars. Bikes. Passion..

Today we serve clients across Nairobi and deliver nationwide across Kenya — from Land Cruiser owners in Karen to road cyclists in Kilimani, to students who want a custom laptop skin in Mombasa.
