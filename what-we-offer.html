Window Tinting & Paint Protection Film (PPF)

PPF (Paint Protection Film) is a clear, self-healing film applied directly over your car's paintwork — permanently protecting against stone chips, scratches, bird droppings, and UV fade without changing how the car looks. Window tinting blocks heat, reduces glare, and adds privacy. Both services are applied on-site at our Parklands shop.
